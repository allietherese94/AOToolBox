Have some feedback? Have you found a bug?

If you have any feedback about our app, or have found a bug in the system, please let us know! Contact Damien Caillaud at animal.observer@gorillafund.org with your comments and concerns, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

We strongly believe that Animal Observer should remain free to use, but if you like this application and want to help us develop future versions, please support us in any way you can!
